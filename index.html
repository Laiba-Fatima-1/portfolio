CEARA — AI Interview Evaluation
AI System Python ↗ Private Repo

A locally deployable AI platform that evaluates interviews in real time — transcribing speech with Whisper, tracking 9 behavioral signals via MediaPipe, scoring answers with Groq + Llama 3.3, and generating rubric-weighted PDF reports.

  • Real-time Whisper transcription updating every 4s; <15s final processing for 3 min audio
  • 9-signal MediaPipe engine: eye contact, blink rate (EAR), posture, pitch variance via librosa
  • 17-dimension configurable rubric; parallel LLM scoring with radar charts in PDF output

  • Zero audio/video transmitted externally — all processing fully local
